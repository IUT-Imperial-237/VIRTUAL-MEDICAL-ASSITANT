SYMPTOMS





How can you Know someone has a Heart Attack or Stroke? Do you feel any tightness in your chest??

Are your arms weak when you try to raise them? Does you face droop??

Do you have difficulties when speaking or you find it hard to understand others?

What are the main symptoms of Stroke and Heart Attack?






What is Stroke?




Stroke also known as cerebrovascular accident or CVA is a disease that affects the arteries leading to and within the brain. A stroke occurs when a blood vessel that carries oxygen and nutrients to the brain is either blocked by a clot or bursts

A stroke occurs when blood flow to an area of the brain is cut off as such the brain cells begin to die

When brain cells die during a stroke abilities controlled by that area of the brain such as memory and muscle control are lost. A stroke is a medical emergency because strokes can lead to death or permanent distability.There are oppurtunites to treat stroke but that the treatment needs to be started in the first few hours.

There are two types of stroke

  • Hemorrhagic Stroke: caused by a leaked blood vessel
  • Ischemic Stroke: caused when a blood vessel carring blood to the brain is blocked

Symptoms of Stroke







FACE DROOPING

Also known as Facial Paralysis occurs when the nerves that control the muscles in the face are damaged in the brain.One side of the face is numb. When asked to smile, the person's smile looks uneven.Sometimes just the lower half of the face is affected, sometimes the whole side, and sometimes the entire face. The problem might also affect how you move your eyes and/or mouth



ARM WEAKNESS

Stroke affect the way muscles work.The patient may have weakness or stiffness in some of the muscles or lose control of the muscles all together.Most movement problems are caused by weakness in your muscles.Here the person finds it hard to raise both their arms, one arm may drift downward



DIFFICULTY IN SPEECH

Stroke also cause communication problems if muscles in the face, tongue throat are affected.The range of communication somehow will depend on where the brain in the stroke happened and how large an area was damaged. A stroke can affect how you speak, understand speech, read or write.Is the speech of the patient slurred?Is he/she unable to speak or hard to understand? Ask the person to repeat a simple sentence.

If the response to the above questions is YES, he or she may be suffering from stroke. Call 9-1-1 and get them to the hospital immediately!

What is heart Attack?



A heart attack occurs when the flow of oxygen-rich blood to a section of heart muscle suddenly becomes blocked and the heart can't get oxygen. If blood flow isn't restored quickly, the section of the heart muscle begins to die. A heart attack also results when a blood clot completely obstrucs a coronary artery supplying blood to the heart muscle and the heart muscles dies.The blood clot that causes the heart attack usually forms at the site of the rupture of an atherosclerotic, cholesterol plaque on the inner wall of a coronary artery.









What are the symptoms of Heart Attack




DISCOMFORT IN CHEST

Most heart attacks involve discomfort in the center of the chest that lasts more than a few minutes, or it may go away and then return.it can feel like uncomfortable pressure, squeezing, fullness or pain.




SHORTNESS IN BREATH

The heart is involved in transporting oxygen to tissues and removing carbondioxide, problems with the hearth affects breathing as such there is shortness in breath when someone is suffering from heart attack.
