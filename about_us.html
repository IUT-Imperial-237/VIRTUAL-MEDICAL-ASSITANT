INTRODUCTION

Virtual Hospital uses an integrated approach, working with health care professionals across the industry to fill the current gap in health services with telemedecine.Virtual Medical Assistant uses technological advances to bring health care facilites to those who have little or no access to it. Telemedecine allows health care professionals to evaluate diagnose and treat patients at a distance using telecommunications technology.

MISSION STATEMENT

Virtual Medecine Assistant has the aim of assisting patients suffering from stroke, develop innovative health care solutions to provide the right care at the right time. Our priority is to be able to treat patients suffering from stroke in rural areas by efficiently meeting their health needs and requirements.

An idea for change

Virtual Medical Assistant(VMA) was an idea we developed to fulfill the current health needs of our elders, families , and friends in the rural areas. The aim being, to provide affordable and top health care services.

Virtual Medical assistance, improving the health industry

By using virtual communications and technology to offer remote diagnosis and faster access to medical care. Our services therefore will reduce the amount of time a patient has to meet an expert, considerably. VMA maximises the use of existing health care facilities by creating networks of cardiologist and health support staff, ensuring efficient patient follow-up,treatment and health monitoring of stroke patients and their concerns.

Ease and Conviniency

Virtual Medical Assistant provides a lot of conviniency , thus limiting the need for a patient to visit a clinic or healthcare center.With Virtual Medical Assistant we aim to enhance doctor-patient relationship using technology, assisting doctors with exclusive support and patients with enhanced consultation experience.
